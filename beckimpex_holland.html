Beck-impex imports New Holland machinery that has only been used for one year. These nearly new machines have been deployed in farms that renew their tractor fleets every year. This allows us to offer nearly new machines at a very sharp price.

Once we come to an agreement with a client, we ensure that processing goes smoothly:

  • We take care of all import and export documents
  • We take care of the transport of the machinery and deliver it to your front door
  • If you wish so, we can add the options of your choice to the tractors (Front linkage - Front PTO - Joysticks - ...). More information about this service can be found under "Machinery on request"
